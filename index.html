The Beginning — 12 January 2026

I first noticed you on TikTok during a live stream. Something about you stood out immediately. Your presence caught my eye, and I couldn't stop thinking about you.

First Message — Late January 2026

After weeks of gathering the courage, I finally texted you with a simple "Heyy". Your confused but cute response "Heyyyy" made my heart skip a beat. In that moment, everything changed.

3

Your Birthday & My Proposal — 21 January 2026

On your birthday, I did something I'd been dreaming about. I sent you a heartfelt letter at 5:31 AM, pouring out everything I felt. You made me feel valued, heard, cared for, and truly loved for the first time. And in that same day, I asked you to be mine forever.

4

Growing Together

We've had arguments, misunderstandings, and moments of doubt. But what made our relationship unbreakable was our ability to apologize, forgive, and choose each other every single time. Your forgiveness and continued care have only made me love you more.

5

The Hardest Test — 6 Hours Apart

We broke up once, but only for 6 hours. When you came back from school, we talked on a call and found our way back to each other. That moment proved that our connection is stronger than any distance, misunderstanding, or temporary pain. You chose me, and I'll always choose you.

6

Distance Can't Define Us

382 km apart. 4 hours and 17 minutes between Rustenburg and Mpumalanga. But these numbers mean nothing compared to the strength of what we share. Every message, every call, every moment strengthens our bond even more.
